# PC Case Design Project

I'm the proud new owner of an RTX 3060 12G that will soon be put to good use for local compute with Ollama and OpenCode. But my Optiplex PC case neither fit the part nor looked the part, so an upgrade was in order.

-DESIGNED in SolidWorks for ease of assembly (no fasteners outside of base for frame), plentiful airflow (FLUENT validation), efficient load transfer (ANSYS study), and - of course - style (cherry featured most prominently).

-BUILT with Dynatorch CNC plasma cutter, Boss laser cutter, Bridgeport mill, Whacheon lathe, Lincoln MIG welder, and Powermatic table saw.

-TESTED with HFS calipers, my trusty multimeter, and a healthy amount of sudo dmesg and update grub.
